# Mission: Chess Mastery for darkkkkkkk0

## Why
Help an 11-year-old chess player (`darkkkkkkk0`) level up their game, build strong fundamentals, and systematically identify and correct common mistake patterns so they can grow from their current level to their target.

## Success looks like
- Reach a Rapid rating of 1000 on Chess.com.
- Identify and eliminate repetitive tactical blunders (e.g. hanging pieces, simple forks/pins).
- Build a solid, repeatable opening routine for White and Black.
- Successfully review and learn from daily matches using automated analysis tools and Chess.com's game review.

## Constraints
- **Age**: 11 years old. Lessons must be engaging, clear, visual, and avoid overly dry or dense academic jargon.
- **Platform**: Chess.com Premium user. We should complement Chess.com's built-in puzzles, game reviews, and video library rather than trying to replicate them.
- **Frequency**: Plays daily, so lessons and analysis should fit into a daily/weekly routine.

## Out of scope
- Theoretical endgame database memorization (e.g. complex pawnless endings).
- Deep opening lines beyond move 5-8. Focus is on tactical awareness and fundamental principles (center control, piece activity, king safety).
